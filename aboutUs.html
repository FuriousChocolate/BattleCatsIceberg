About Us

This was a project created by me (FuriousChocolate) and some others to create an ultimate compendium of all bc knowladge and lore. It was inspired by other battle cats enthusiasts who had made websites such as Thanksfeanor's UDP or Clamchowder's event data website. I also want to give a big thanks to u/Indiozia for the Battle Cats Iceberg the IVth: Ultimate Edition, as most of the entries in this iceberg were taken directly from that, with our own twists and additions added on. Note that some entries from Indiozia's iceberg have been modified or removed from this iceberg in order to refine it and perfect it more.
